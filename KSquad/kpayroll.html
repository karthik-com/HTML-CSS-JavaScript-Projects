KPayroll

KPayroll is an end-to-end HRMS and payroll management software with link to all the required features like Employee On-boarding and management, Advances, Leaves, Shifts Management, Deductions, Incentives, Attendance management with Biometric Device integration, Payroll generation, Payroll Taxes and filing of Returns, Pay-slip generation and Record Keeping. Payroll administration is one of the most important and essential activities that a company performs. It is not easy to manage payroll process as is it the most time-consuming daily activity. kPayroll is the robust payroll management software helps you to automate the payroll process, so that you can focus on your operations and daily core tasks.
